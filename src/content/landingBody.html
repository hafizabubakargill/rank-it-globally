FAQ

Questions We Get Every Day.

Google AI Overviews pull from pages with clear structure, strong entity signals, and schema markup. We build sites that speak AI's language: semantic HTML, FAQ schema, clean content hierarchy, and authoritative signals. Clients have landed in AI Overviews within weeks of launch.
Traditional SEO targets the 10 blue links. AI SEO (also called AEO — Answer Engine Optimization) targets AI-generated summaries at the top of Google, ChatGPT search, Perplexity, and Gemini. Both matter. We do both — optimising your pages to rank in classic results AND to be cited by AI models as the authoritative source.
Local keywords typically move within 60–90 days. Competitive niches take 3–6 months. Nobody honest promises overnight rankings. What we do promise: every page is built for maximum crawlability from day one — technical SEO, schema, content hierarchy — so Google has every reason to rank you as fast as possible.
Our landing pages start at $699, brand websites at $1,299, and e-commerce builds at $1,499. Every project includes mobile-first design, speed optimisation, and on-page SEO. Use the price estimator above to get an instant quote for your specific needs.
Yes — when the landing page converts. Most small businesses waste ad spend by sending paid traffic to a generic homepage. We pair Google Ads management with high-converting landing pages so every click has the best chance of becoming a customer: lower cost-per-lead, faster ROI, campaigns that pay for themselves.
A redesign done right improves four things: speed (Core Web Vitals), trust (professional look and social proof), conversion (clearer CTAs, less friction), and SEO (clean code, proper structure). We preserve your existing rankings during migration so you don't lose traffic while gaining a better site.
Local rankings come down to three factors: Google Business Profile optimisation, NAP consistency across citations, and on-site local signals (location pages, schema, local keywords). Our Local SEO plan covers all three — we audit your presence, fix inconsistencies, and build the signals Google needs to put you in the local 3-pack.
CRO is getting more leads from the traffic you already have — without spending more on ads. If you're getting visitors but not conversions, CRO is usually the highest-ROI move. We run heatmap analysis, audit your funnel, restructure page layout, and test changes that move the needle on actual revenue.
